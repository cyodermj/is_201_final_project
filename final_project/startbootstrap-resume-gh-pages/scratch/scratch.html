How to Increase Your Bench Press

A simple beginner's guide to getting stronger and lifting more.

1. Proper Form

Having proper form is essential to prevent injury and make progress.

  1. Setup:
    • Lie flat on the bench
    • Grip slightly wider than shoulders
    • Feet flat
    • Shoulder blades retracted
  2. Execution:
    • Unrack bar over chest
    • Lower to chest
    • Press up to full extension
